## Design Recommendations for Zwanga

### Color Scheme
- **Primary Color**: #FF6B35 (Orange - from your app theme)
- **Secondary Color**: #F7B801 (Yellow/Gold)
- **Background**: White (#FFFFFF) or gradient from #FF6B35 to #F7B801
- **Text Color**: White or Dark Gray (#1F2937)

### Content Elements to Include

1. **App Logo/Icon**
   - Place Zwanga logo prominently (left or center)
   - Size: ~150-200px height
   - Position: Left side or center-top

2. **Main Headline**
   - "Zwanga" (large, bold)
   - "Ride-Sharing in Kinshasa" or "Smart Carpooling"
   - Font: Bold, Sans-serif (Montserrat, Inter, or similar)
   - Size: 60-80px for "Zwanga", 30-40px for tagline

### Typography
- **Primary Font**: Bold, Modern Sans-serif
- **Secondary Font**: Medium weight for tagline
- **Hierarchy**: Clear size differences between elements

### Assets Needed

- Zwanga logo (high resolution, transparent background)

### Design Checklist

- [ ] Dimensions exactly 1024Ã—500px
- [ ] Logo is clear and readable
- [ ] Text is legible at small sizes
- [ ] Colors match brand identity
- [ ] No important content near edges (safe zone)
- [ ] File size under 15 MB
- [ ] Exported as PNG (preferred) or JPEG
- [ ] Tested on different screen sizes

### Safe Zone

Keep important content (logo, main text) within:
- **Top**: 50px from top
- **Bottom**: 50px from bottom
- **Sides**: 50px from left/right

This ensures content isn't cut off on different devices.
